    Image Menagement
    From this page you can see all approved and awaiting approval pictures for all plants and manage them.

    A approved image that has no plant linked to it will go in the general gallery that is visible on all plant pages

    and qr linked pages that have no pictures for their plant.

    About Images without a plant:
    When a plant dies or is deleted for other reasons all pictures of that plant will be detached and go in the general gallery

    that can be managed from here.
